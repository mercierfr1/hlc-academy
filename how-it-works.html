How much time do I need each day?

+

We recommend 20-30 minutes per day for optimal learning. You can do this all at once or split it into smaller sessions throughout the day. The key is consistency, not duration.

What if I fall behind on the curriculum?

+

No worries! Our curriculum is self-paced. You can take as much time as you need on each module. Many students take 16-20 weeks to complete the 12-week curriculum, and that's perfectly fine.

Do I need expensive trading software?

+

Not at all! We teach you to use free platforms like TradingView and MetaTrader. You can start practicing with paper trading accounts before risking real money.

Can I access the content on my phone?

Absolutely! Our mobile app gives you full access to all content. Many students prefer learning on mobile during commutes or breaks.

You maintain access to all content and the community. We also offer advanced modules and ongoing market analysis to keep your skills sharp.
